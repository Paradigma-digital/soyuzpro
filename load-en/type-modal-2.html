Flight stair towers for public events

01

Description

The easy and safe lifting to heights sometimes is an integral part of construction and operation of facilities as well as of public cultural events. The structural concept is based on both standard members, which you can use for any scaffolding types, and special reinforced beams of flight stairs, which ensure fast installation, a high load-carrying capacity, and safe operation of flight stairs. Standard steel decking for scaffolding serves as steps and can be used later as boarding of the working tier, for instance, on facade scaffolding. In contrast to compact aluminium flight stairs, flight stairs for public events are made of steel and distinguished by their high load-carrying capacity in order to ensure safe operation during periods with large crowds.
