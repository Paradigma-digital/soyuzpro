Dock systems for aircrafts, seacrafts, and other craft types

01

Description

This is a special scaffolding type to be used along bodies of various-purpose craft. These can be aircrafts, ships, submarines, spacecrafts, and many other vehicle types that have complex geometry of the stately body. Such access systems can be either stationary or mobile-based. This is typically an individual project to be developed in detail by our Engineering Department, with negotiation of a whole lot of technical parameters, e.g., such as the maximum permissible distance to the body, the quantity of working tiers and their distribution in height, a possibility of resting on or fastening to the craft body, and many more. During development, the project may require an additional service of 3D scanning of the real-life craft body to be scaffolded in order to avoid inaccuracies in the technical documentation and to eliminate any probability of error. Such structures have high use flexibility and can be refitted for different geometry of the body with partial dismantling of the already finished scaffolding, for instance, using cantilever members. We apply only a state-of-the-art and professional approach, which allows various dock systems to be operated in an efficient and safe manner.
