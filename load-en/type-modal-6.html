Mobile wheeled scaffolding (scaffold towers)

01

Description

In some cases, mobile scaffolding allows you to significantly save time and money for scaffolding installation (up to 10 times or more). Our specialists will study your scaffolding requirement in detail and, if possible, offer mobile wheeled scaffolding. Dimensions of this scaffolding can be really impressive, up to 30 or more metres in height. Our engineers carefully calculate such designs. You may rest assured that this scaffolding type is safe and stable. The mobile scaffolding type is also fully versatile and requires no special members. The wheelbase of mobile scaffolding is an analogue of a screw jack and is easily adjustable in height within 55 cm, which allows the height of the working tier to be perfectly fitted for your needs. A special version of wheels designed for rolling the structures on rails (guides) is available as special solutions, which is especially relevant for tunnels or places where strict geometry of scaffolding movement is of utmost importance. In addition, special heavy-duty wheels, with a capacity of 5 or more tonnes per wheel, can be designed upon your request.
