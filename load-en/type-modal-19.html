Description

It is hard to imagine a major show event without either temporary prefabricated demountable or fixed stands for an audience. Thanks to our own production facility and Engineering Department, we manufacture stands of any complexity and criticality, including telescopic stands. It is also noteworthy that, in addition to manufacture of stands, we produce seats and chairs. We provide integrated turnkey solutions to ensure maximum comfort for our customers.
