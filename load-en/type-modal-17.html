Light and sound

01

Description

Hardly any public cultural event can do without light and sound portals. Standard members of «SOYUZ» SmartScaff series scaffolding include reinforced girders as well as trusses (wedge-type and off-system) to fasten light and sound arrays with various weights to them. You can select required scaffolding members yourself based on the load-carrying capacity according to the «SOYUZ» Technical Brochure, or our Engineering Department will develop and calculate your portal. It is also necessary to make is possible to cover portals against an environmental exposure (rain, wind, snow, etc.) in order to protect expensive equipment. To do so, we have developed a portal cover system, which can be installed quickly and safely—the same as SmartScaff series scaffolding—and guarantees reliable weather protection.
