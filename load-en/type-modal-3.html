Description

Thanks to special members that join separate poles into pole groups, a phenomenal load-carrying capacity can be achieved to redistribute the support of bridge spans, heavy metal trusses, and tunnel roofs as well as for other purposes that require transfer of enormous loads to temporary supports. The load-carrying capacity of only one group can reach 20 (!) tonnes. Such projects are calculated in detail by our engineers, and then careful installation and follow-up control are carried out under the capable guidance of supervisors. If necessary, we will design and manufacture special members so as to meet any challenge we face with regard to transfer of high loads to support scaffolds.
