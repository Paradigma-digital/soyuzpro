Due to high-speed assembly of the load-bearing framework of «SOYUZ» SmartScaff series scaffolding, they are indispensable when holding any public cultural and show events, particularly in large cities, when the time for installation of structures is very limited. An extra feature of shows and events is that they have a specific date that cannot be postponed or rescheduled. Our customers may count on delivery of «SOYUZ» scaffolding right on time due to a large warehouse stock of scaffolding (from 1,000 tonnes) as well as wealth of experience (including international) gained immediately in delivery and installation of audience arenas since 2004.
