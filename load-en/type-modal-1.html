Compact flight stairs

01

Description

The easy and safe lifting to heights is an integral part of construction and operation of facilities. The structural concept is based on both standard members, which you can use for any scaffolding types, and prefabricated aluminium stair flights with barrier railings, which ensure fast installation as well as easy and safe operation. It should be noted that flight stairs can be either used as free-standing structures for lifting to buildings and facilities or built into the scaffolding structure in order to ensure easy access to scaffolding, for example, on scaffolding of facade, suspended, bird-cage, and other types.

02

Technical characteristics

Flight length: 22.5/3 m

Flight width: 0.75 m

Height: 100 m (calculation and a design are required)

Load: 200 kg/m²
