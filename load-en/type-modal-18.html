Athletic facilities

01

Description

In confirmation of application flexibility and variability of «SOYUZ» scaffolding systems, they can also be used as sports structures and facilities. For example, these can be complex climbing frameworks, huge ramps/slopes for skiing and snowboarding competitions, supporting structures for BASE jumping or water jumps, jumping and landing hills, and many other structures for athletic use, both in competitions and events and in day-to-day training life. All scaffolding members are protected by hot-dip galvanising with a coating thickness of 60 to 80 µm, which reliably protects steel pipes against corrosion at an exposure to the environment and ensures the scaffolding service life of 20 years!
