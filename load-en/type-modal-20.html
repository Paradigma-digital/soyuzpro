Cantilever scaffolding

01

Description

This scaffolding is symbiosis between two types—cantilever scaffolding and common supported scaffolding. The cantilever portion of this scaffolding is used as a support, which can be brought out at any height of the building/structure. This allows you to save significantly on works and scaffolding members, without erecting a structure from the ground level. This approach is especially relevant in case of great heights and local works. Similar scaffolding is often used at industrial enterprises in confined spaces with a lot of process equipment. Like all cantilever scaffolding, it should be designed and used only by professionally trained personnel. With this type of scaffolding, it is crucial to correctly prop or calculate the amount of ballast/counterweight at fixations of the cantilever portion.
