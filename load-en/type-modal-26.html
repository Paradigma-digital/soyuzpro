Description

The SmartScaff system makes it possible to assemble podiums with various geometries and functionalities. Wide modularity of dimensions and the possibility to manufacture special members allow our customers to set their creativity free in selection of solutions and to implement their ideas as they were originally conceived, without engineering constraints with respect to the podium’s possibilities. The load-bearing framework of the podium is wedge-coupled modular scaffolding made of galvanised steel with a high yield point, which enables our podiums to perceive truly high loads, up to 7 tonnes per pole, and this value can be even higher, if necessary!
