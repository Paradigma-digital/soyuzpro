Mobile craned

01

Description

One of particular mobile-scaffolding solutions is to relocate the assembled scaffolding structure with a crane. This solution makes it possible to significantly decrease the budget for installation and occasionally for purchase of scaffolding. Both small towers and large scaffolding structure assemblies, for instance, ready-made bridge crossings with connected flight stairs, can be relocated with a crane! When performing such operations, particular attention should be paid to arrangement of diagonals and slinging points as crane-assisted relocation fully changes the static operation scheme of the structure and redistributes forces between scaffolding members. «SOYUZ» engineers in such projects perform all necessary technical support both remotely and immediately at the customer’s site. During crane-assisted relocation, poles shall be interconnected similarly to suspended scaffolding, and screw jacks shall be fixed using special holders in order to prevent falling out.
