Facade scaffolding

01

Description

A classic scaffolding type is facade scaffolding often seen by each of us on the streets of towns and cities. Due to high precision of manufacture and handpicked materials, our facade scaffolding can be assembled quickly and can reach over 100 metres in height. Due to flexibility of the scaffolding system, even the most complex facade shapes, including those with a reverse slope or significantly projecting parts, can be easily scaffolded. If necessary, our engineers will develop a project for scaffolding installation and will support it until the facility is completed. You can also order extra members for scaffold anchoring/fastening to a wall so as to have the entire project executed by us on a turnkey basis.
